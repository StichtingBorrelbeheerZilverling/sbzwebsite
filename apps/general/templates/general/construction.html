Structure

The purpose of Stichting Borrelbeheer Zilverling (SBZ) is the purchase and sale of drinks in the drinking rooms managed by the foundation.

The six participating associations in SBZ, and therefore the board of the foundation, are:

  • I.C.T.S.V. Inter-Actief, the study association for the bachelors Technical Computer Science and Business Information Technology and associated masters
  • W.S.G. Abacus, the study association for the bachelor and master Applied Mathematics
  • E.T.S.V. Scintilla, the study association for the bachelor Electrical Engineering and the master Electrical Engineering Systems & Control and Embedded Systems
  • S.A. Proto, the study association for the bachelor Creative Technology and master Interaction Technology
  • S.V. Stress, the study association for the bachelors Business Administration, International Business Administration, Industrial Engineering and Management, and Business Information Technology and their associated masters
  • S.A. Communiqué, the study association for the bachelor Communication Science and corresponding master

The general board of the foundation can be reached via ab@sbz.utwente.nl.
